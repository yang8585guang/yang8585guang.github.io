Biography

I am a Ph.D. student under the supervisor of Prof. Kun Qian at School of Biomedical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My doctoral thesis is to develop the novel micro-/nano-materials for supporting the metabolic analysis by mass spectrometry towards clinical applications of diagnostics and monitoring.

Interests
  • Interaction between nanomaterials and metabolites
  • Metabolomic (metabolic) analysis
  • Electron microscopy
